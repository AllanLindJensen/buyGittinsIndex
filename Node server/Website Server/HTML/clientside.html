The discount factor

Getting a succes next time, the service is tried does have a value. Not the same as getting a succes this time, but maybe 90% of it. Then you discount factor is 90%

You may have found a good service, 2 successes against 1 failure. It may proove advantageous to try a new service, so that you eventually will finde a better one. How high your discount factor be, for this to be the case?
